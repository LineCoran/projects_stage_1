How it works

The main goal is to help the animals, as well as the nature reserves and zoos where they are kept. We are currently working on video projects targeting pandas in China, eagles on an island near Los Angeles, allingatoes in Florida and gorrilas in the Congo. These have a total of more than 1,500 mammals and reptiles.
